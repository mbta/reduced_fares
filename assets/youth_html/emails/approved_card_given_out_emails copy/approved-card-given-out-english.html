About Your Youth Pass

Thanks for picking up your card at the Admin: Pick Up Program Name(no title) Youth Pass office.

  • You can use your Youth Pass for:
    • 50% off one-ways fares on bus, subway, Express Bus, Commuter Rail, and ferry.
    • $30 monthly passes or $10 7-day passes for unlimited bus, subway, Commuter Rail Zone 1A, and Inner Harbor Ferry rides.
    • Discounted monthly passes for Express Bus, Commuter Rail, and ferry.
  • If your card is damaged, lost, or stolen, you can request a replacement card.
  • If you are receiving a replacement card and have a balance or a monthly pass on your old card, you must visit the CharlieCard Store in person to transfer value to your new card. You must have your old and new serial card numbers to make the transfer. If you don't have a record of your old serial card number, contact your local Youth Pass office.
  • Your Youth Pass is valid through October 31, Personal Info: Program Year for Reporting(no title). You can submit an application for a new Youth Pass starting October Personal Info: Program Year for Reporting(no title).
