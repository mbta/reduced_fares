Apply for a Blind Access CharlieCard

Before You Begin

This application is for people who are legally blind.

You will need to upload the following three items:

  1. A government-issued photo ID, such as a passport or state ID. Expired IDs will not be accepted.
  2. A recent color picture of yourself from the neck up in front of a solid, light-colored background. You cannot wear a hat, face covering, or sunglasses.
  3. A Massachusetts Commission for the Blind ID card or Certificate of Blindness, or other blindness certification.

Upload Photo ID

If uploading a photo ID isn't a good option for you, we recommend applying in person at the CharlieCard Store.

Photo ID

Upload a Photo of Yourself

Upload a recent color picture of yourself from the neck up in front of a solid, light-colored background. You cannot wear a hat, face covering, or sunglasses.

Guidelines:

  • Submit a color photo, taken in last 6 months.
  • Use a clear image of your face. Do not use filters commonly used on social media.
  • Use a solid, light-colored background with no background objects.

How your photo should look

  • ✔ Solid, light background
  • ✔ Neck up
  • ✔ Religious head coverings accepted
  • ✔ Glasses without glare

Mistakes to avoid

  • ✗ Busy background
  • ✗ Wearing hat
  • ✗ Not from neck up
  • ✗ Filter
  • ✗ Dark background
